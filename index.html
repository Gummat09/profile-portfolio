About

Hello! I'm Sfundo Gumede, a passionate web designer and web developer based in Durban, South Africa. With a robust skill set in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and fundamental knowledge of C# and React, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to my projects.

My experience extends across various platforms, including WordPress, Shopify, and Webflow, ensuring I can deliver seamless and responsive web solutions tailored to diverse client needs. Whether crafting intuitive user interfaces or developing dynamic web applications, my goal is to create engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. Let's collaborate to bring your vision to life!
